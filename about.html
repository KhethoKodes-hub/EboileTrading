Our Roots

FROM LOCAL
START TO
REGIONAL FORCE

Eboile Trading began in 2012 as a small catering cooperative in Nairobi. Driven by a vision to deliver excellence across industries, we expanded into cleaning and transport by 2015, and into general merchandise supply and civil construction shortly after.

Today we stand as a trusted multi-service partner — blending the agility of a local team with the capacity to handle national-scale projects. Each division carries the same founding spirit: hands-on quality, transparent relationships, and results that outlast the contract.
